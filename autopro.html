AUTO PRO

AUTO PRO

Create a Type Specimen to showcase the Typeface Auto Pro

Auto Pro is incredibly diverse with 24 fonts in the family. My Type Specimen consists of three tabbed sections detailing the specifics of Auto Pro. I created mioré patterns, a 2D animation technique, which animates the tabs as you pull them. I feel this interactive motion best highlights the diversity of the font. To further showcase the vast quantity of typefaces I designed an accompanying glyphset.
