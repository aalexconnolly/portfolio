BAFFLED?

design and code a website which aids parents in learning about online safety

The main theme of my title was interconnectivity through time. The film revolves around a series of characters connected by ephemera. I illustrated this through pieces of paper that over lap and form the credits however the paper, like the characters, never properly meet. I wanted to capture the atmosphere of a scrapbook, or bits and pieces that get left behind.
